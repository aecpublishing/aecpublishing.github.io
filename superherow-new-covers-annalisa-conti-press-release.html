New York, July 25, 2018 - Annalisa Conti lives and writes in New York City, where she has been spending the last few years of her life. She is a modern nerdy feminist who injects her rational mindset and rocket science (really!) background in the world of Women’s Fiction. She writes about women in today’s world: awake, fierce, heroes. Sometimes, superheroes.

The first ten episodes of her short stories THE W SERIES will be updated, from August 2018, with captivating new cover art byValeria Frustaci, an italian illustrator and graphic designer. Valeria, after earning her degree in Architecture, specialized in Illustration and Visual Design in Milan and has worked with many publishing houses.

In 2013, 2014 and 2018 she was selected for the Italian Society of Illustrator's Annual.

The W Series is available as ebook on Amazon, Barnes & Noble, Google Play, and Apple Store.
